Dat'AK

My Journey in the World of Data.

Hello, I'm Akdi Oussama, a 21-year-old engineering student at École Centrale Méditerranée. In 2020, I completed my high school with a mathematics background, and then I spent two years in Preparatory Classes for Grandes Écoles (CPGE). Afterward, I joined École Centrale Méditerranée.

I've always been fascinated by computers and data, especially artificial intelligence. This curiosity has pushed me to keep learning and growing in these areas.

I also conduct computer workshops for middle school students, collaborating with Magic Makers, a program that teaches computer science.

I created Dat'AK to share my journey and data projects. It's a place where I can learn and grow while also sharing what I've learned with others. I hope this website can inspire and help you on your own journey.

Welcome to Dat'AK, where we explore the exciting world of data science and artificial intelligence together.
