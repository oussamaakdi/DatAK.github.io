Credit Customer Analysis for Smart Decision-Making

Description

Step into the world of data-driven credit customer analysis, where we're putting our focus on binary classification for precise credit decisions.

Feature Engineering

we've invested in feature engineering, tailoring features to maximize the separation between the two classes within our dataset. These meticulously designed features are the key to significantly enhancing the accuracy of our classification models.

Diverse Model

we have many different models available to use in that case. From the reliability of linear regression to the robustness of ridge regression and the flexibility of multi-layer perceptrons (MLPs). Tree-based models round out our toolkit, ensuring a comprehensive exploration of classification avenues.

Precision through Grid Search CV

Central to our pursuit of precise and efficient model performance is the Grid Search Cross-Validation (Grid Search CV) technique. It's more than just a tool; it's a systematic approach to fine-tuning our models. By systematically seeking out the best hyperparameters for each model, we're enhancing their accuracy and reliability.

Understanding used Models

Linear Regression

A very powerful linear model in the world of regression analysis, linear regression models the relationship between the target variable and the independent features with linear equations.

Ridge Regression

Building upon linear regression, ridge regression introduces L2 regularization, helping to prevent overfitting and improving model stability.

Multi-Layer Perceptrons (MLPs)

These artificial neural networks consist of multiple layers of interconnected neurons, capable of solving complex nonlinear problems, making them invaluable in this project.

Tree-Based Models

Leveraging decision trees or ensemble methods like random forests and gradient boosting, tree-based models provide flexibility and power in classification tasks.
