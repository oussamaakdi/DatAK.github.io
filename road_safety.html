Analyzing UK Road Safety Data 2016

Description

In this project, we're immersing ourselves in the UK-2016-Road-Safety dataset, with a mission to uncover the riskiest hours of the day. This analysis not only helps us identify potentially hazardous hours but also empowers us to predict safe hours in new scenarios.

Data Exploration

Exploration of the dataset, covering various tables, including vehicle, accident, and casualties. This exploration is key to unraveling insights into road safety and understanding the dataset's features

Data Preprocessing

To create a comprehensive dataset, we merge various tables, ensuring all the relevant information is in one place. Handling missing values, exploring correlations, and applying scaling techniques contribute to a refined dataset that sets the stage for powerful analysis.

Diverse Model Application

A range from dependable linear models to the precision of Support Vector Machines (SVM) and the strength of ensemble models and sequential models. Each model adds a unique perspective to our analysis.

Integrating a Powerful Dashboard

we're integrating a dynamic dashboard into this project. This dashboard isn't just an aesthetic choice; it's a functional tool for data analysis and decision-making. It's designed to provide clarity and aid in recognizing patterns and insights within the data, adding a layer of interactivity to our exploratory.
