Diabetes Predictive Analysis

Description

In this project, we've used the potential of data analysis to predict diabetes outcomes. The work began with a dataset sourced from Kaggle, where we embarked on a multi-step process to unlock meaningful insights.

Data Exploration

Our first step involved delving into the dataset's columns through data exploration. Here, we aimed to comprehend the various features and their influence on predicting diabetes. Uncovering the persistence of specific features in diabetes prediction was a pivotal aspect of this stage.

Feature Selection

To enhance model performance, we employed a covariance matrix for feature selection. This process enabled us to eliminate correlated columns.

Data Preprocessing

Data preprocessing played a crucial role in our project. We prepared the data by encoding categorical features and divided it into distinct training and test sets. This step ensured that our models could effectively learn and generalize from the data.

Power BI Dashboard

Adding a powerful dimension to our project, we integrated a Power BI dashboard. This dashboard offers a dynamic and visual platform for data analysis and decision-making. It aids in recognizing patterns, relationships, and insights within the data. Its utilization is particularly valuable during the data exploratory phase.
